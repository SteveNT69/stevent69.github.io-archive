Cookies

These are some pretty basic cookies, but they're still cookies I guess.

Ingredients

  • 220g butter
  • 110g sugar
  • 270g flour

Recipe

  1. Get a fucking bowl and chuck the ingredients in
  2. Mix the ingredients with a spoon
  3. Use your hands to form a dough with the mixture
  4. Roll the dough into cookie shapes on a tray (use a star cutter or whatever the fuck you want)
  5. Bake them in the oven for around 10 minutes at 190°C (374°F for you Americans)
  6. If you didn't burn the house down, enjoy!
